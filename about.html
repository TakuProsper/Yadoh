About Us

Est 2013

Our Story

Yadoh Trading company is a wholly owned Zimbabwean company established in 2013 with the aim of becoming a leading force in the energy Industry.

In 2015 Yadoh Trading Company established a dedicated department for supplying of electricals, hardware and protective equipment to satisfy demands by clients

A decade on, our journey remains resolute:
Today, Yadoh stands as the premier choice for electrical contracting and hardware supply. Our clients entrust us for our unwavering dedication to quality and safety, establishing us as a steadfast leader in the industry.

Our Values

Innovation and Aggression
Equity, Fairness and Transparency
Customer comes first

Our Vision

To continuously give a world class high voltage substations and powerline construction, hardware products and services, and be a trusted partner who will always deliver on high voltage rquipment, building and allied materials.

Our Mission

To provide world class, durable and exceptional products and services backed by knowledgable consultants who provide a seasoned and well informed view on current trends in high voltage substations, power lines, building and allied materials.

Our Security Principles

Beyong Zero: At our core, we believe that safety management transcends the goal of zero harm. We are dedicated to identifying and correcting all at-risk behaviors and unsafe work conditions, ensuring a safe and healthy environment for everyone.

Active Involvement Of All: We engage our entire workforce, including sub-contractors, to identify and mitigate risk behaviors and conditions, fostering a safe and responsible work environment.

Actively Caring For People: We cultivate a culture of care, valuing every individual we interact with, both within and outside our organization, as vital to our success.

Non-Negotiable SHE StandardsWe operate by simple, law-abiding standards and regulations, ensuring compliance and integrity in all we do.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to holding our leaders, managers, and supervisors accountable for exemplary safety leadership and the effective management of Safety, Health, Environment, and Quality (SHEQ). Ensuring the safety of our personnel across all business operations is our top priority.

Our Beliefs

At Yadoh Trading Co, we prioritize SHEQ (Safety, Health, Environment, and Quality) in all our operations. We commit to:
- Protect the health and safety of everyone involved with our assets and activities
- Minimize our environmental impact

We strive for zero injuries and consistently apply world-class safety standards across our business. To achieve this, we've established a SHEQ management system and appointed a dedicated SHEQ head to lead and monitor our compliance.
