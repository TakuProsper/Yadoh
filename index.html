Who are we?

Yadoh Trading Company, founded in 2013, is a proudly Zimbabwean-owned enterprise dedicated to excelling in the energy industry. Over the years, we’ve expanded our focus to supply a wide range of electrical, hardware, and protective equipment. As the most trusted electrical contractor and supplier of high-quality hardware in Zimbabwe, we are committed to delivering world-class, durable, and innovative products and services to our clients.

Our vision is to be a trusted partner in high voltage substations and powerline construction, while also providing essential hardware products and services that our clients can rely on. Guided by our core values—innovation, transparency, and customer-centric service—we aim to contribute positively to the communities and industries we serve. With a strong foundation in Safety, Health, Environment, and Quality (SHEQ), we uphold the highest safety standards to ensure a responsible and sustainable impact in all our operations.
