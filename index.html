Who are we?

At Yadoh Trading Company, we are a team dedicated to safety, productivity, quality, and compliance. Our focus revolves around the well-being of our employees and clients, with safety being our top priority. Through strict adherence to safety protocols, continuous investment in employee training, and the maintenance of hazard-free environments, we ensure a secure workplace for all.

Our commitment to productivity drives us to embrace innovative technologies and optimize processes for efficient results. Quality is at the core of our operations, as we consistently exceed customer expectations through rigorous quality control measures. Additionally, we pride ourselves on our unwavering compliance with legal and regulatory standards. These values define us and guide us in delivering unmatched service, fostering enduring partnerships built on trust, reliability, and excellence.

Our SHEQ Vision

To conduct our business with zero harm to the employees, stakeholders and communities that we engage with.

To continually give a world class product and service

To be trusted partner who will always deliver on all high voltage substation and line, construction, building and allied materials

Our Beliefs

Yadoh Trading Company considers the managements of SHEQ matters to be integral part of the management of its business, therefore, so far as is reasonable and practical we undertake to:-

> Protect the health and safety of all persons involved with or affected by our activities

> Minimize the environmental impact of all our operations. All injuries are preventable and will ensure that world class safety standards are applied consistently across our entire business. In support of this, Yadoh Trading has:
1. Established on SHEQ management system based on nationally accepted standards.
2. Head of SHEQ to coordinate all matters relating to SHE and to provide the implementation and review compliance with the policy.

Our Safety Principles

Beyong Zero: At our core, we believe that safety management transcends the goal of zero harm. We are dedicated to identifying and correcting all at-risk behaviors and unsafe work conditions, ensuring a safe and healthy environment for everyone.

Active Involvement Of All: We engage our entire workforce, including sub-contractors, to identify and mitigate risk behaviors and conditions, fostering a safe and responsible work environment.

Actively Caring For People: We cultivate a culture of care, valuing every individual we interact with, both within and outside our organization, as vital to our success.

Non-Negotiable SHE StandardsWe operate by simple, law-abiding standards and regulations, ensuring compliance and integrity in all we do.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to holding our leaders, managers, and supervisors accountable for exemplary safety leadership and the effective management of Safety, Health, Environment, and Quality (SHEQ). Ensuring the safety of our personnel across all business operations is our top priority.
